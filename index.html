About

Roy is a Portfolio Manager based in Singapore with a background in quantitative finance and computing. His expertise includes the research, development, and implementation of algorithmic trading strategies. This includes working with cross-functional teams and third-party service providers to develop and setup related infrastructure to support these strategies. Roy is also experienced in the areas of software development, cybersecurity, programming languages and technology.

Roy developed a strong interest for computers in his youth, designing blog websites and writing scripts for fun. Some of the software he has written includes video games, code analysis tools and multimedia applications. In his free time, Roy loves to tinker with technology, go for long runs and walks around Singapore, and read about new scientific discoveries.

Roy holds a Master of Computing in Information Systems and a Bachelor of Computing (Honours) in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore. He also holds a Diploma in Digital Entertainment Technology from Nanyang Polytechnic, Singapore.
